Development

AmeriSouth has developed two new construction TDHCA developments, but the emphasis remains on the rehabilitation of existing apartment communities. Over the years, the company has completed acquisition rehabilitation projects on over 40 apartment communities. In addition to the expertise in acquisition rehabilitation projects, the company specializes in the restoration of its own apartment communities.



Property Management

AmeriSouth's focus remains on delivering quality places to live in the affordable rent market - continually improving product quality while reducing costs through unique efficiency enchancements.
AmeriSouth has been specializing in hands-on multifamily property management for over 28 years. The organization has managed more than 60 apartment complexes and over 11,000 units.
The AmeriSouth organization has controlled over 25 properties with HUD HAP contracts or involving Low Income Housing Tax Credits (LIHTC). Many of AmeriSouth's properties have community programs including after-school programs, a Montessori school, affiliation with local churches, and regular community events.
AmeriSouth's management team has combined unparalleled experience with the relentless energy of a young company to become a recognized leader in the affordable apartment industry.
